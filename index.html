Relaxation Techniques

Breathing Exercise

  • Sit or Lie Comfortably: Find a quiet place where you can sit or lie down comfortably without distractions.

  • Inhale Slowly: Close your eyes. Inhale quietly through your nose for a count of 4.

  • Hold Your Breath: Hold your breath for a count of 7.

  • Exhale Slowly: Exhale completely through your mouth, making a whooshing sound for a count of 8.

Study Break Tips

Take 5-minute breaks every 25 minutes...

Stretch or Move: Do a quick stretch or walk.

Hydrate & Snack: Drink water or eat a light snack.

Power Nap: Take a 10-20 minute nap if needed.

Mindfulness: Practice a brief mindfulness or meditation session.
